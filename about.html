About Kramps EHS

Your trusted partner in safety, compliance, and environmental stewardship.

Overview

Kramps EHS Ventures is a leading independent multidisciplinary company offering a comprehensive range of specialized engineering and management services in Environmental, Occupational Hygiene, Health, and Safety (EHS).

Our world-class consultants are drawn from various engineering and management disciplines, including Environmental and Safety Engineering, Geological Engineering and Hydrology, Occupational Hygiene, Process Control Engineering and Simulation, and Geomatics, Artificial Intelligence, and Geodesy.

Our Aim: To help you manage risk and ensure continual improvement, driving sustainable performance and ensuring business continuity free from legal interference.

Our Mission

To provide our clients with robust, innovative, cost-effective, and tailored guidelines for approval processes, alongside technical advice to offset adverse environmental, health, and safety risks.

Our Vision

To become the pre-eminent environmental, health, and safety engineering services provider within the Sub-Saharan African region.
